## Visual Takeaways

- `full_attention_velocity` is visibly worse on T1 and T2: it edits broadly and
  changes subject identity or object placement more than the semantic/SAM
  variants.
- `full_source_v_inject` improves preservation proxies and DINO source
  similarity on this small slice, but the CLIP target delta is negative and the
  qualitative grids still show weak edits on T3 and mixed edit strength on
  T1/T2. Treat it as an experimental preservation control, not as the main
  method.
- T3 remains a color-edit failure/weak-edit case across variants. The semantic
  car support is too broad and triggers `mask_guard`, while the final visible
  car body remains mostly yellow.

## Interpretation Guard

These ablations are seed-10 only. They are useful for diagnosing the current
system and for a workshop-style ablation table, but they should not be framed
as statistically stable evidence without additional seeds or stronger semantic
edit-success metrics.
